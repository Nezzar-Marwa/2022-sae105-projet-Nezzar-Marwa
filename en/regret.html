A regret of our generation

The rising of bullying

nowadays in school one child out of 10 is a victim of bullying that makes one million children who suffer the consequences and this bullying is increasing and continues even after school on the internet with cyber bullying. Thousands of children are victim of this because of their differences, some are discriminated for their disabilities, others are harassed for their sexuality, gender identity and even for their fashion style, this harassment can be moral or even physical, in the active world at work women are mostly affected by the harassment (sexual or not) even outside of work they can suffer, in July 2020 81% of women have declared being victim of street harassment

The rising of racism

In 2020 in the United States after the black lives matter demonstrations racist acts increased by 40% without any real decisions or solutions being found, many victims after the death of George Floyd were discovered. racism against asian people increased due to COVID, denunciations have risen sharply. Racism against native Americans also increased native women are the first victims with black women of abduction and racism. A law is even about to pass. This law aims to lift the prohibition of the law of protection of native children. In Canada the last reserves of the natives are deprived of clean and drinkable water Many other racist acts are increasing

The rising of sexism

In 2019 in France 87% of the victims of sexist crimes are women. The wage gap for equal work and diploma is 9% In 2022 there are already 79 women victims of femicide in France. All these acts are increasing, In the United States the law that allowed access to abortion in all states has been questioned and banned in several states however it will not stop the abortions it will just endanger the lives of women who will still get abortions into illegal clinics with no sanitary measures.

The rising of anti lgbt acts

In the United States a law that prohibits students from speaking about gender identity or sexuality has been passed that means that awareness campaigns about harassment related to lgbtphobia are also prohibited the consequences are negative since many children will not be able to understand their identity or others’ identity and thus their safety won’t be guaranteed.
