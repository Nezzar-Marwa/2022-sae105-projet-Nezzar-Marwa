A movie to see once in a lifetime

K-12: A phantasmagoric adventure

We follow the story of Crybaby, a child with a very difficult family background. Her and her

friend Angelita, have incredible abilities and they are starting their year of school in a sleepaway school. At first glance this school seems like the perfect place to learn. However we will soon realize how corrupted

this school and its people are and how stressful it is for our two protagonists who will have thanks to their new friends and tenacity destroy this system and school who only seek to condition and exploit them, and they will also try to find their place in a world that rejects them for their differences

A reflect of society

The main theme is difference and learning to accept and be proud of yourself in a world that does not like difference. The film will also denounce

discrimination that humans can face in their lives because of their difference with sub-themes and parallels with real life. Indeed, a parallel was made

with racism and the black lives matter demonstrations that took place during the 2020 covid crisis. Discrimination against LGBT people are also mentioned with one of the teachers being fired because she is transgender. The director will also

talk to us about sexism and in particular the rape culture and the gender norms imposed to women. Other themes such as manipulation or psychiatric disorders are also talked about with one of the main

characters being manipulated by a teacher in exchange for favors or a student who suffers from eating disorders.

“You hear that? Liberty and justice for all? That’s bullshit.”

A very creative director

Melanie martinez née le 28 avril 1995 à New york, est une chanteuse une réalisatrice et une actrice américaine elle est connu pour avoir participé à the voice en interprétant toxic de britney spears en 2015,

elle à écrit son premier album cry baby dans lequel elle raconte l'histoire de crybaby un personnage qui la représente, en 2019, elle sort son second album K-12 accompagné du film du même nom. Elle est

aussi connue pour ses œuvres inspirées du surréalisme pop avec une apparence assez enfantine qui cachent en fait des thèmes plus sombres et sérieux.

A fantastic detour

c'est le premier film qu'elle réalise, elle à réalisé plusieurs clip de musique et une compilation de tous ses clips suivant l'histoire et l’univers de l'album.

les tenues et l’ambiance globale certains éléments du décors, tout en s’inspirant aussi du film alice de Jan Svankmajer qui nous montre un côté un peu plus étrange et nous fait ressentir un malaise,
